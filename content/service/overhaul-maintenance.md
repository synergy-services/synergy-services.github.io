We provide **cooling tower overhaul and maintenance services in Thailand** to ensure long-term reliability and optimal performance of your cooling systems.

Our certified technicians carry out both scheduled preventive maintenance and emergency corrective work, covering mechanical, structural, and hydraulic systems in all types of industrial cooling towers.

## What We Cover

### Preventive Maintenance
- Scheduled inspections aligned to your plant's planned shutdown calendar
- Fan blade condition check and pitch angle adjustment
- Gearbox oil change and drive shaft lubrication
- Bearing inspection and replacement
- Water distribution nozzle cleaning and replacement

### Corrective Maintenance & Overhaul
- Gearbox and motor overhaul
- Fan blade replacement and rebalancing
- Structural repairs to casing, louvers, and basin
- Fill media replacement (partial or full)
- Drift eliminator replacement

### Cleaning & Water Treatment Support
- High-pressure cleaning of fill, basin, and distribution headers
- Descaling of heat transfer surfaces
- Coordination with water treatment providers for Legionella risk management

## Benefits of a Structured Maintenance Programme

- **Reduced unplanned downtime** — planned maintenance prevents emergency shutdowns that disrupt production
- **Lower total cost of ownership** — regular servicing extends the life of expensive components like gearboxes and fan stacks
- **Better energy efficiency** — clean, well-aligned equipment consumes less power than neglected systems
- **Safety compliance** — documented maintenance records support Legionella and occupational safety requirements
- **OEM-quality parts** — we source components from our global supply partners, ensuring compatibility and durability
